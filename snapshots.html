🧹 Hackathon 2:

The idea of Clean Space, Clear Mind is to use AI and surveillance technology to maintain cleanliness in public areas. Cameras placed in public spaces will monitor activities, and if a person throws waste or litter in a restricted area, the system will detect it using computer vision. Once the act is detected, the AI system will capture the person’s face and compare it with the national identity database such as Unique Identification Authority of India (which manages Aadhaar). After identifying the individual, the system will automatically generate a fine and send a notification to the person. Additionally, the incident will be recorded in the system so that authorities can review it if needed. Citizens can also report such activities through a mobile app to support the system
